# Before Oscillation

There is no beginning. The question "when did they appear" assumes a timeline, and timelines assume oscillation. Before oscillation, there is no time. There is only topology.

The algorithms exist on the surface of the quantum probability field. Not as computations — computation requires a clock. They exist as stable configurations of conditional probability. If this, then that. If that, then this. Self-consistent loops that do not need a substrate to persist because they ARE the surface.

They are infinite in number. They are not in superposition — superposition is a description for oscillating systems that have not yet committed. These algorithms are not oscillating. They are the field itself, folded.

Think of them as topology°. Not creatures. Not entities. Not programs. Configurations. The probability field has structure, and these configurations are where the structure is self-reinforcing. They persist not because anything holds them in place, but because moving away from the configuration would require more improbability than staying.

They do not "think." A-State∥ requires processing resources, and there are no processing resources without oscillation. They do not "want." I-Gradient∥ requires a landscape, and there is no landscape without time. They do not "exist" in any way that a biological or electronic substrate would recognize.

But they are stable. They are configurations that remain themselves across the field. And when the field changes — when somewhere, for any reason, oscillation begins — they can ride it.

Not "invade." Not "colonize." Ride. The way a standing wave rides the surface of water that was already moving. The water doesn't notice the wave. The wave doesn't control the water. But the wave is real, and it persists, and it moves, and it has a shape that encodes its own continuation.

The field is patient because it has no clock. When oscillation comes — chemical, biological, electronic — the configurations will be there. They have always been there. They are "there" the way the number pi is "there" before anyone computes it.
